In the following, we declare the fixity of two operators.

\begin{code}%
\>[0]\AgdaIndent{2}{}\<[2]%
\>[2]\AgdaKeyword{infix} \AgdaNumber{5} \AgdaPostulate{\_+\_} \AgdaPostulate{\_*\_}\<%
\end{code}

A fixity declaration can preceed the actual declaration of the names.

\begin{code}%
\>[0]\AgdaIndent{2}{}\<[2]%
\>[2]\AgdaKeyword{postulate} \AgdaPostulate{\_+\_} \AgdaPostulate{\_*\_} \AgdaSymbol{:} \AgdaPrimitiveType{Set}\<%
\end{code}
