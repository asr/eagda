In the following, we declare the fixity of two operators.

\begin{code}%
\>[0][@{}l@{\AgdaIndent{1}}]%
\>[2]\AgdaKeyword{infix}\AgdaSpace{}%
\AgdaNumber{5}\AgdaSpace{}%
\AgdaPostulate{\_+\_}\AgdaSpace{}%
\AgdaPostulate{\_*\_}\<%
\end{code}

A fixity declaration can preceed the actual declaration of the names.

\begin{code}%
\>[0][@{}l@{\AgdaIndent{2}}]%
\>[2]\AgdaKeyword{postulate}\AgdaSpace{}%
\AgdaPostulate{\_+\_}\AgdaSpace{}%
\AgdaPostulate{\_*\_}\AgdaSpace{}%
\AgdaSymbol{:}\AgdaSpace{}%
\AgdaPrimitiveType{Set}\<%
\end{code}
